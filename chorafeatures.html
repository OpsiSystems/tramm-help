About Chora

GPU-Accelerated

Chora uses GPU processing to evaluate large numbers of route combinations simultaneously, far faster than traditional CPU-based solvers.

Built for Real-World Complexity

Handles the constraints that make real logistics hard — time windows, capacities, vehicle types, and multi-stop routes.

Developed by Opsi

Chora is developed entirely in-house by the Opsi engineering team, giving Tramm full control over solver behaviour and future enhancements.
